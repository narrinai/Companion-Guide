Dream Companion FAQ

What is Dream Companion?

Dream Companion is an NSFW AI roleplay platform designed for immersive experiences, offering users the ability to create and chat with AI companions for roleplay and more, while also enabling the generation of images and videos for a truly realistic feel.

How much does Dream Companion cost?

Dream Companion offers a free tier with limited features. Premium plans start at $11.99/month (or $5.84/month billed yearly). The Ultimate plan is $44.99/month (or $24.99/month billed yearly) with unlimited messaging.

Is Dream Companion safe to use?

Dream Companion uses Stripe for payment processing and states they don't store credit card details. They implement security protocols, though users should review the privacy policy and use appropriate precautions with NSFW platforms.

What makes Dream Companion unique?

Dream Companion stands out through its combination of custom character creation, NSFW video generation, uncensored in-chat images, long-term memory system, and sensual audio messages for immersive AI roleplay experiences.

Can I use Dream Companion on mobile devices?

Dream Companion works through web browsers on mobile devices, though it's not optimized for mobile. There is currently no dedicated mobile app available.

Does Dream Companion remember our conversations?

Yes, Dream Companion features a long-term memory system that remembers your preferences, past interactions, and relationship history to create continuity across conversations.

Can I create custom characters on Dream Companion?

Yes, Dream Companion allows extensive character customization including appearance, personality traits, communication style, interests, and roleplay scenarios.

What are Dream Coins and how do they work?

Dream Coins are the platform's currency used for image and video generation. Premium plans include 100 coins/month, Ultimate plans include 800 coins/month. Additional coins can be purchased if needed.

Does Dream Companion support NSFW content?

Yes, Dream Companion is specifically designed for NSFW AI roleplay with no censorship filters. It supports adult conversations, explicit image generation, and NSFW video creation.

How can I cancel my Dream Companion subscription?

To cancel your Dream Companion subscription, go to Profile Settings and click 'cancel subscription'. You can also contact info@mydreamcompanion.com for assistance.
