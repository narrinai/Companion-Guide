What is Janitor AI?

Janitor AI is an uncensored AI character platform that allows users to engage in conversations without the typical content restrictions found on other platforms. Designed for mature audiences, it offers complete creative freedom for roleplay scenarios, NSFW content, and adult conversations.

The platform operates on an API-based system, allowing users to connect their own AI models (like OpenAI's GPT or other providers) for chat functionality. This approach gives users control over their AI experience while maintaining the uncensored nature that sets Janitor AI apart from mainstream alternatives.
