What is Claude AI?

Claude AI is Anthropic's flagship AI assistant, designed with a focus on being helpful, harmless, and honest. Built for productivity and professional use, Claude excels at complex reasoning, writing, analysis, and coding tasks while maintaining strong safety principles and ethical guidelines.

The platform is particularly known for its large context window, allowing users to work with extensive documents and maintain long, coherent conversations. Claude's training emphasizes responsible AI development, making it a trusted choice for businesses, researchers, and individuals seeking reliable AI assistance.
