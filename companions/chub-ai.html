What is Chub AI?

Chub AI is a platform that allows you to interact with characters through the use of Artificial Intelligence Large Language Models (LLMs). These models are accessed through APIs which Chub streamlines into a convenient chatbot service with advanced features like chat trees, image generation, and import/export capabilities.

Chub is completely uncensored. They do not restrict the variety of characters that can be posted on the platform nor the type of interactions you can have with said characters. This makes it ideal for users seeking creative freedom in their AI roleplay experiences without content limitations.

The platform supports numerous APIs including OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Anthropic (Claude), along with their own powerful models like Soji 671B - multiple times the size of models used on similar platforms. Chub also supports the development of free, uncensored AI models and regularly updates their offerings.

Chub AI FAQ

What is Chub AI?

Chub AI is an uncensored AI character platform that allows you to interact with characters through advanced Large Language Models (LLMs). It features character creation, chat trees, image generation, and complete creative freedom without content restrictions.

How much does Chub AI cost?

Chub AI offers a free trial with limited messages, Mercury tier at $5/month for casual use with smaller models, and Mars tier at $20/month for unlimited access to powerful models like Soji 671B.

Is Chub AI uncensored?

Yes, Chub AI is completely uncensored. They do not restrict the variety of characters that can be posted or the type of interactions you can have. The platform is designed for adults (18+) seeking creative freedom.

What makes Chub AI unique?

Chub AI stands out with access to Soji 671B - a model multiple times larger than competitors offer. It also features OpenAI API compatibility, chat trees for branching storylines, and supports the development of free, uncensored AI models.

Is there a free trial for Chub AI?

Yes, Chub AI offers one-time free trial credits amounting to a few dozen messages, sent to new users regularly. This allows you to test the platform before subscribing.

What does 'Unlimited' mean on Chub AI?

Unlimited on Chub AI means unlimited for a single user, similar to Netflix. Concurrent requests from the same account return a 429 error. Obvious key sharing will result in usage restrictions.

Can I use Chub AI's API elsewhere?

Yes, Chub AI's API can be used anywhere that supports OpenAI's APIs. This makes it versatile for integration into various applications and projects.

Can I pay with cryptocurrency?

Yes, Chub AI accepts cryptocurrency payments in 4-month increments as an alternative to traditional payment methods.

How does Chub AI compare to other AI companions?

Chub AI offers competitive features in the AI companion space with an 8.2/10 rating. It excels in model quality and API flexibility, competing well with platforms like SpicyChat, Janitor AI, and Crushon AI.

Can I create my own characters on Chub AI?

Yes, Chub AI offers comprehensive character creation tools. You can create custom characters with detailed personalities, backstories, and behaviors. The platform also allows importing/exporting characters and chats from other services.
