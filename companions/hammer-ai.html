What is Hammer AI?

Hammer AI is a freemium AI character chat platform that offers unlimited conversations in its free tier, with optional paid upgrades for enhanced features. This privacy-focused platform provides users with access to both local and cloud-based AI models, ensuring flexibility in how you interact with your AI companions.

With a generous free plan that includes unlimited conversations and characters, Hammer AI makes AI companionship accessible to everyone. The paid tiers unlock additional cloud LLMs, higher image generation limits, and larger context windows for more sophisticated conversations, making it ideal for both casual users and power users.
