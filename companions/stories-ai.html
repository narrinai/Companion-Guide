Overview

Stories AI is a privacy-first roleplay AI chat platform that stands out in the crowded AI companion market with its commitment to user privacy and sustainable business model. Unlike many competitors, Stories doesn't train on your data, doesn't monitor your chats, and operates without VC backing - meaning they're here to stay.

The platform offers a refreshing pay-as-you-go model with a generous free chat allowance, eliminating the need for subscriptions. With support for both NSFW and SFW content, multiple personas, and advanced features like lorebooks and group chats, Stories AI caters to serious roleplayers who value both privacy and flexibility.

User Experience

Stories AI delivers a refreshingly straightforward user experience that prioritizes functionality over flashy features. The interface is clean and intuitive, making it easy to jump into conversations without a learning curve. The platform's focus on privacy means you can engage freely without worrying about your data being used for training or your conversations being monitored.

The pay-as-you-go model is particularly user-friendly, eliminating subscription fatigue while still providing a generous free tier for casual users. The ability to create multiple personas and engage in group chats adds depth to the roleplay experience, while the lorebook feature allows for rich worldbuilding that enhances immersion.

The magic import tool is a standout feature, recognizing that users may want to bring their existing characters from other platforms. This shows a user-first mentality that's refreshing in the AI companion space.

Privacy & Safety

Stories AI takes a strong stance on privacy, which is increasingly rare in the AI companion space. They explicitly state that they don't train on user data or monitor chats, providing a level of privacy that many competitors can't match. This privacy-first approach extends to their business model - without VC backing, they're not pressured to monetize user data.

While the platform allows NSFW content with minimal censorship, they maintain clear prohibited content policies to ensure safety. The option to create public, private, or unlisted chatbots gives users control over their content visibility.

Final Verdict

4.0 / 5

Stories AI is a solid choice for privacy-conscious users who want a flexible, uncensored AI roleplay platform without the commitment of a subscription. The pay-as-you-go model, combined with advanced features like lorebooks and group chats, makes it particularly attractive for serious roleplayers.

While it may not have the polish or user base of larger platforms, its commitment to privacy, sustainability, and community-driven development makes it a refreshing alternative in a market dominated by VC-backed companies. If you value privacy and want to support a sustainable, user-focused platform, Stories AI is definitely worth trying.
