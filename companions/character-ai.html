What is Character.AI?

Character.AI is the world's largest AI character chat platform, allowing users to chat with millions of AI-powered characters created by the community. From fictional characters and celebrities to original personalities, Character.AI offers the most diverse selection of AI companions available.

Founded by former Google AI researchers, Character.AI uses advanced language models to create engaging, contextual conversations that feel natural and entertaining. The platform has become the go-to destination for character roleplay and creative AI interactions.
