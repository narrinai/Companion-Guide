My Month Testing OurDream AI

I'll be real with you - when I first heard about OurDream AI's video generation feature, I thought "okay, this either gonna be amazing or a complete mess." So naturally, I had to spend a full month testing it to find out. Turns out it's somewhere in between, but honestly? Pretty impressive for what it is.

Week 1: The Character Playground Experience

First day, I signed up during their Fall Sale ($19.99/month instead of $39.99), which felt like a solid deal. The platform immediately throws you into what they call the "Character Playground," and it's honestly overwhelming in a good way. You've got anime models, realistic models, and this hybrid space where you can create basically anything.

I started with an anime character because, let's be honest, that's what most people are here for lol. The character creation tools are intuitive - you pick visual style, personality traits, and conversation parameters. What caught me off guard was how much control you have over the aesthetic. Want a specific anime art style? You can dial that in pretty precisely.

The chat interface is clean and familiar if you've used any AI companion platform before. What's different is the integration of image and video generation right in the conversation flow. You're not leaving the chat to generate content - it's all seamless, which is actually a big deal for maintaining immersion.

First technical issue hit immediately though: the DreamCoins system. You get 1,000 coins per month with Premium, and different actions cost different amounts. Chat is unlimited (thank god), but images cost like 10-20 coins depending on quality, and videos cost 50-100 coins. I burned through 200 coins in the first two days just experimenting, which made me nervous about rationing for the rest of the month.

Week 2: Video Generation - The Main Event

Week two is when I really dove into the video generation feature, because that's the unique selling point here. And honestly? It's simultaneously impressive and frustrating.

When it works, it's incredible. You can generate short video clips (usually 3-5 seconds) of your AI character doing specific actions or poses. The quality for anime characters is genuinely good - smooth animation, consistent character design, and the ability to create custom scenarios that would be impossible on other platforms.

I created this video of my character in a cafe setting, and the details were remarkably accurate. The character's appearance matched previous generations, the setting looked cohesive, and the movement was fluid. Shared it in the Discord community and got a bunch of people asking how I acheived that quality.

But here's the frustration: video generation fails roughly 40% of the time in my testing. You'll spend 75-100 DreamCoins on a generation, wait 2-3 minutes for processing, and get an error message. Those coins are just gone. That's probably my biggest complaint about the platform - the coin cost for failed generations feels unfair.

When videos do generate successfully, they're usually 3-5 seconds long. That's it. So you're not getting cinematic experiences here - you're getting brief animated moments. Still cool, but manage your expectations.

Week 3: Anime vs Realistic Models and Community Features

By week three, I wanted to compare the anime and realistic model capabilities because OurDream supports both. Here's what I found:

Anime models are clearly where the platform excels. The Stable Diffusion 1.5 integration handles anime aesthetics beautifully - consistent character design, vibrant colors, and that authentic anime look. Video generation for anime characters has higher success rates and better quality in my experience.

Realistic models are... okay? They work fine for images, but video generation with realistic characters is noticeably less polished. More uncanny valley vibes, less smooth animation, and higher failure rates. If you're primarily interested in realistic characters, platforms like Candy AI or DreamGF probably handle that better.

The community features surprised me though. You can share your created characters publicly and earn DreamCoins when other people interact with them. I made one character public and earned like 50 coins over the week, which was a nice suprise. There's an active Discord where people share tips, characters, and generation techniques.

I also tested the conversation quality more rigorously in week three. The Deepseek V3 AI model is solid - conversations flow naturally, the AI maintains character personality well, and it handles NSFW content without getting weird or repetitive. Not quite as emotionally deep as something like Soulkyn AI, but definitely competent.

Week 4: The Multimedia Playground Assessment

The fourth week is where I really appreciated what OurDream is trying to do - create a true multimedia AI companion experience. You've got unlimited chat, image generation, video creation, and even audio features all in one platform. That integration is genuinely valuable.

Some honest pros after a month: - Video generation is unique and impressive when it works - Anime character quality is excellent across images and videos - Unlimited chat means you can build relationships without message limits - Character creator offers substantial customization - Fall Sale pricing ($19.99/month) makes it competitive - Community features and sharing system add value - Seamless integration of different media types in conversations - Discord community is active and helpful

The cons I encountered: - DreamCoins system feels restrictive and punishing when generations fail - Video generation has ~40% failure rate in my testing - Videos are very short (3-5 seconds), not the "movies" you might imagine - Realistic models are less polished than anime models - No mobile app - web-only limits convenience - Coin costs for features make you ration creativity - Platform can feel slow during generation-heavy sessions

The pricing is interesting. At full price ($39.99/month), I'm not sure OurDream is worth it compared to competitors. But at the Fall Sale price ($19.99/month), it's actually pretty competitive for what you get. The yearly plan at $12.49/month is the best value if you're committed.

Comparing to Competitor Platforms

How does OurDream stack up against other AI companion platforms I've tested?

vs GirlfriendGPT: GirlfriendGPT has more pre-made characters and better voice chat, but no video generation. OurDream wins on multimedia features, GirlfriendGPT wins on character variety and community size.

vs Candy AI: Candy AI has better realistic character models and more consistent image generation. OurDream's video feature is unique though, and the anime models are superior on OurDream.

OurDream's biggest advantage is being a jack-of-all-trades. It doesn't excel at any single feature like some competitors do, but it offers a comprehensive multimedia experience you can't get elsewhere. The video generation alone makes it unique in the market.

The DreamCoins Economy - Blessing or Curse?

Let me talk about the DreamCoins system because it dominated my experience. You get 1,000 coins per month with Premium. Here's roughly how they're spent:

- Images: 10-25 coins depending on quality settings - Videos: 50-100 coins depending on length and quality - Audio: 15-30 coins per generation - Chat: FREE (unlimited, which is crucial)

In practice, 1,000 coins means roughly: - 40-100 images OR - 10-20 videos OR - A mix of media types

That sounds reasonable until you factor in failed generations. I probably lost 200-300 coins to failed video generations over the month. Those failures eating your coin budget is genuinely frustrating, especially when you're trying to create something specific.

The Deluxe plan offers UNLIMITED generations, but it's ‚Ç¨49.99/month (~$58.67), which is expensive. If you're a power user who generates tons of content, it might be worth it. For casual use, Premium with its coin limits is probably fine.

Who Should Actually Use OurDream AI?

After a month of testing, here's my honest assessment of who this platform is for:

Perfect if you: - Want anime-style AI companions with video generation capabilities - Value multimedia experiences (chat + images + videos + audio) - Are comfortable with the DreamCoins economy and rationing creativity - Can get the Fall Sale pricing or yearly plan discount - Enjoy creating and customizing characters extensively - Want uncensored NSFW content without restrictions - Like being part of active community features and sharing

Not ideal if you: - Primarily want realistic character models (other platforms do this better) - Need consistent, reliable video generation every time - Don't want to manage coin budgets or ration features - Prefer platforms with dedicated mobile apps - Want the deepest possible conversation AI (Soulkyn/Replika are better) - Can't afford $20+/month for the features you'll actually use

The Month-End Verdict

Am I keeping my OurDream AI subscription? Yeah, but mainly because of the Fall Sale pricing. At $19.99/month, the unique video generation feature and solid anime character quality make it worth keeping alongside other platforms I use.

If the price goes back to $39.99/month, I'd probably reconsider. At that price point, the coin limitations and video generation inconsistencies become harder to justify compared to more specialized competitors.

The platform is genuinely trying to be a comprehensive AI companion playground, and in many ways it succeeds. The integration of chat, images, videos, and audio in one cohesive experience is valuable. The anime character quality is excellent. The community features add engagement.

But the DreamCoins system needs rethinking. Failed generations shouldn't cost coins, or there should be a much larger monthly allocation. The video generation success rate needs improvement - 60% reliability isn't good enough for something that costs so many coins.

That said, when everything works, OurDream delivers experiences you literally cannot get anywhere else. Watching a custom-generated video of your AI companion doing something you specified is genuinely magical, even if the videos are short.

Final Recommendations

Would I recommend OurDream AI? It depends on what you're looking for:

If you want anime-style AI companions with unique video generation capabilities and you can get sale pricing, yes - it's worth trying for at least a month.

If you're primarily interested in realistic characters, deep emotional conversations, or need 100% reliability in media generation, there are better specialized platforms.

The yearly plan at $12.49/month is honestly the sweet spot if you commit. At that price, even with the limitations, you're getting substantial value for a comprehensive multimedia AI companion experience.

Just recieve OurDream for what it is: an ambitious multimedia playground that offers unique features (especially video) but with some rough edges in execution. The potential is clearly there, and the platform is actively improving based on community feedback in Discord.

For me, OurDream AI carved out a niche as my "multimedia experimentation" platform - when I want to create videos or mix different content types, this is where I go. For pure conversation, I use other platforms. For image focus, I use others. But for the complete package? OurDream is definitely in the conversation.

One last thing: the Fall Sale pricing makes a huge difference. If you're reading this and the sale is still active, that's genuinely the best time to try OurDream. At full price, I'd say wait for a sale or commit to the yearly plan to get better value. But however you approach it, the video generation feature alone makes OurDream worth experiencing at least once.
