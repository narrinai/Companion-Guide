What is OurDream AI?

OurDream AI is an innovative AI companion playground platform that allows users to create, customize, and interact with AI characters through multiple formats including unlimited chat, image generation, and video creation. The platform positions itself as a comprehensive "AI Companion Playground" where users can bring their digital companions to life.

The platform combines character creation tools with advanced AI technologies, using Stable Diffusion 1.5 for image generation and Deepseek V3 as the chat model. OurDream AI supports both anime and realistic character models, offering users creative freedom to design and interact with personalized AI companions across different media formats.
