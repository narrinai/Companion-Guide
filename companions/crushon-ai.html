Crushon AI FAQ

What is Crushon AI?

Crushon AI is an uncensored NSFW AI character chat platform that allows users to create and interact with AI characters without content restrictions. It offers unlimited character creation and access to community-generated characters for roleplay scenarios.

How much does Crushon AI cost?

Crushon AI offers a free tier with 50 messages per day. Premium plans start at $4.99/month for unlimited messaging and advanced features, with higher tiers at $9.99/month including image generation.

Is Crushon AI truly uncensored?

Yes, Crushon AI is known for being one of the few truly uncensored AI chat platforms. Unlike many competitors, it does not filter NSFW content or restrict adult conversations, allowing users complete freedom in their interactions.

Can I create my own characters on Crushon AI?

Crushon AI offers unlimited character creation capabilities. Users can design custom AI characters with detailed personalities, appearances, backstories, and conversation styles tailored to their preferences.

Does Crushon AI have a mobile app?

Currently, Crushon AI is a web-based platform accessible through browsers on desktop and mobile devices. There is no dedicated mobile app available at this time, though the website is mobile-responsive.

What makes Crushon AI different from Character.AI?

Unlike Character.AI which heavily filters NSFW content, Crushon AI is specifically designed for uncensored adult conversations. It allows NSFW roleplay and content that would be restricted on mainstream platforms, making it popular among users seeking unrestricted AI interactions.

Are there community-created characters on Crushon AI?

Yes, Crushon AI features a large library of community-created characters spanning diverse categories and scenarios. Users can browse, chat with, and customize these characters, or create their own from scratch.

Does Crushon AI remember past conversations?

Crushon AI includes memory features that allow characters to remember context from previous conversations. The quality and extent of memory retention improves with premium subscription tiers.

What is the message limit on the free tier?

The free tier of Crushon AI provides 50 messages per day. For unlimited messaging, users need to upgrade to a premium subscription starting at $4.99/month.

Is Crushon AI safe and private?

Crushon AI implements standard privacy and security measures for user data protection. However, as with any online platform, users should avoid sharing sensitive personal information and review the privacy policy before use.
