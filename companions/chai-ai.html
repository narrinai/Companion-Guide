What is Chai AI?

Chai AI is a mobile-first AI character chat platform designed specifically for smartphone users. With a focus on community-created personalities and interactive conversation games, Chai AI offers a unique blend of entertainment and social interaction through AI characters.

The platform stands out with its mobile-optimized interface, gamified chat experiences, and strong community aspect where users can discover, rate, and interact with thousands of AI characters created by other community members. Chai AI emphasizes fun, casual conversations and interactive entertainment.
