What is Kajiwoto AI?

Kajiwoto AI is a unique platform that allows users to create their own AI companions called "Kajis" using custom training data and personalities. Unlike other platforms that offer pre-made characters, Kajiwoto focuses on giving users complete control over their AI companion's development and behavior.

The platform features private and public chat rooms, group conversations with multiple Kajis, and advanced customization tools. Users can train their Kajis with personal datasets, creating truly personalized AI companions that reflect their specific preferences and conversation styles.
