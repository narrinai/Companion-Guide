Replika AI App Accused of Deceptive Marketing

Federal Trade Commission complaint alleges deceptive marketing practices by popular AI companion app Replika, raising questions about transparency in the AI companion industry.

FTC Complaint Details

The Federal Trade Commission has received a formal complaint against Replika, one of the most popular AI companion platforms, alleging deceptive marketing practices in how the company presents its services and capabilities to users.

Industry Implications

This complaint highlights growing scrutiny of AI companion platforms and their marketing practices, potentially setting precedents for industry regulation and transparency requirements.
