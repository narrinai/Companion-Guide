‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What are video AI companions?

Video AI companions are artificial intelligence-powered virtual companions that incorporate video capabilities into their interactions. This includes AI-generated videos, real-time video calls, personalized video messages, and interactive video scenarios. Video AI companions combine advanced conversation AI with visual experiences for more immersive relationships.

How realistic are AI-generated videos?

Modern AI video generation has reached impressive levels of realism. Top platforms like Secrets AI and Ourdream AI create hyper-realistic videos with natural movements, realistic facial expressions, and high-resolution quality. However, careful observation may still reveal AI artifacts like occasional unnatural movements or lighting inconsistencies. Technology continues improving rapidly.

Do I need to pay for video features?

Yes, video generation and video call features typically require premium subscriptions due to high computational costs. Most platforms offer free tiers with text chat and images, but video capabilities start at $9.99-$19.99/month depending on the platform and video feature set.
