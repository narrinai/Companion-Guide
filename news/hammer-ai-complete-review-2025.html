Local Models vs Cloud Models Comparison

One of Hammer AI's unique features is support for both local and cloud-based AI models. This technical distinction has significant implications for privacy, speed, and conversation quality.

Cloud Models (Default)

  • Pros: Faster responses, more sophisticated AI, better conversation flow
  • Cons: Requires internet connection, conversations processed on remote servers
  • Best For: General chat, complex roleplay, creative writing

Local Models (Advanced Option)

  • Pros: Complete privacy, no data leaves your device, works offline
  • Cons: Requires more powerful hardware, slower response times, simpler conversations
  • Best For: Maximum privacy, sensitive conversations, testing

For most users, cloud models provide the best experience. However, the local model option demonstrates Hammer AI's commitment to privacy and user choice.
