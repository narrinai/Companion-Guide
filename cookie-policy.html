What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

CompanionGuide.ai uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand where our visitors are coming from.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language preferences) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may impact your overall user experience.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Third-Party Opt-Outs: You can opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies at aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu

Note: Blocking or deleting cookies may prevent you from using certain features of our website and may affect your browsing experience.
